The following list of lakes, reservoirs and ponds are managed as put and take trout fisheries. These are stocked frequently with catchable-sized trout. The regulations follow Maximum Possession limits and are below.Provincial Regulations for Put and Take Stocked Lakes● OPEN all year● Bait is allowed● 5 trout of any size● 3 Northern Pike of any size● 15 Yellow Perch of any size● For other species, see Maximum Possession Limits.Stocked Waterbodies with Special RegulationsSome recreational stocked waterbodies are managed with special regulations, such as catch-and-release, reduced bag limits, fishing seasons, and minimum size limits. These regulations help ensure fish are available for harvest in the waterbody or support Quality Stocked Fisheries that provide opportunities to catch and/or keep a “memorable” sized fish.These waterbodies are listed in the Watershed Units’ regulation tables beginning on page 30 and are identified by a fish symbol. Unless otherwise noted in the site specific table, regulations for other species follow provincial put‑and‑take stocked lake regulations; although species limits are shown for clarity, not all species may be present in each stocked lake.Stocking can vary from year to year.
